Taco Bowl

Description

This Taco Bowl recipe includes proteins such as ground beef & chicken. Followed by some carbs and veggies!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Black Beans
  • White Rice
  • Corn
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Sliced Tomatos
  • Lime
  • Fajita Sauce
  • Ground Beef Seasoning

Steps

  1. Prep/chop all veggies before cooking
  2. Heat up pan on medium-high heat
  3. Add chicken to pan with Fajita Sauce, continue to cook until done
  4. Add Ground Beef to pan with Seasoning
  5. Cook Rice
  6. Add food into bowl as one!
