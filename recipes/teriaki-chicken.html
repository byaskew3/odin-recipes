Teriaki Chicken Bowl

Description

A healthy and tasty meal to build muscle and burn fat!

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • Rice
  • Broccali
  • Teriaki Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium-high heat
  2. Add shredded chicken to pan, cook until well-done.
  3. Steam broccali
  4. Cook rice
  5. Add all ingredients to bowl
  6. Drizzle teriaki sauce over the top
