Healthy Breakfast

Description

This Healthy Breakfast recipe allows you to fuel your body for the day and getting your calories maintained!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Turkey Bacon
  • Sausage Link
  • Sugar-free Syrup

Steps

  1. Wisk 3 eggs in a bowl
  2. Heat Air Fryer to 400 degrees
  3. Heat up skillet on medium heat
  4. Dip each side of bread into egg bowl
  5. Place bread onto skillet, cook on each side for 1-2min
  6. Once Air Fryer is pre-heated, Insert turkey bacon and sausage link for 8min.(Flip them when 3min remain)
  7. Cook scrambled or sunny-side up eggs
